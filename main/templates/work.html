Projects

Glimma Ai

At Glimma AI, I was the sole developer working on a prototype along with the founders that successfully attracted investor funding and led to my promotion to Lead Developer, where I established the tech stack and built a development team to bring our product to the beta testing phase.

Home 2 Retail

Home 2 Retail (H2R) is a fully functional e-commerce platform I developed that enables users to create their own brand page and effortlessly upload and sell products. This platform simplifies online selling for non-technical individuals by allowing them to list their goods through a single, easy-to-use form.

Fit Ai

I created an application that leverages the Strava API to gather my recent training data and the GPT API to analyze this information. The app provides personalized exercise and nutritional advice, as well as recommendations on areas to focus on for improvement based on my Strava statistics.

Mindfull Reminders

As part of a team for a Hackathon, I developed an application focused on mindfulness. This basic application provides users with reminders to follow the 20-20-20 rule during work, displays desk exercises, and shares inspirational quotes to promote well-being and productivity.

Guess The Boozer (Pub!)

A social media site where users could post vague images of their locations, primarily pubs and bars, and challenge others to guess where they were. Users could create accounts, like, follow, and comment on each other's posts, fostering an interactive and engaging community.

Code Hero

I developed a game similar to Guitar Hero or Rockband, where users test their typing and coding skills by ensuring correct syntax passes through a designated point. This interactive game challenges players to improve their coding accuracy and speed in a fun, engaging way.
